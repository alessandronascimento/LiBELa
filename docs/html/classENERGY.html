Detailed Description

The class ENERGY contains three methods dedicated to Binding Energy computation. Here we implemented a method to compute a typical VDW term (AMBER FF Style), typical electrostatic term (Coulomb potential), and soft-core potentials for both VDW and electrostatic terms.
